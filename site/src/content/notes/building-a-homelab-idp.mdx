## Why build an IDP at home?

The best way to understand platform engineering is to build the platform yourself. No managed services, no training wheels — just bare metal, open source, and a lot of YAML.

## Stack overview

- **Compute**: Proxmox VMs on mini PCs (Acemagic, Beelink)
- **Orchestration**: K3s (lightweight Kubernetes)
- **Networking**: Headscale VPN mesh + Traefik ingress
- **Auth**: Authelia with OIDC for SSO
- **Observability**: Grafana + Loki + Prometheus
- **IaC**: Terraform (DNS), Ansible (provisioning), Kustomize (K8s)
